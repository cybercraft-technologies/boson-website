About Us

Boson Academy is a startup by a group of college graduates of the premier Institutes in India, including IITs, AIIMSs, and IISERs. We at Boson Academy aim to bridge the economic gap between quality entrance coaching and students. We plan to provide students with cost-efficient entrance packages. Our specialty will be well educated and qualified faculties.

We guarantee the best teaching methodology and a motivating environment. We can shed light on these premier institute’s features through our experiences; this will be more impactful and encouraging for the students rather than a third-person narrative. We believe that quality education is for all, and it shouldn’t be limited to the economically privileged section of society.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create an educational environment where every student can get a superior education, paving the way to their dreams, disregarding their economic background.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help the students in all aspects of their learning process, starting from conceptual clarity to mastering problem solving, thus empowering them to perform at higher stages.

Why Choose Our Institution

Tutors from Premier Institutes of India

High standard in Teaching, inculcating modern pedagogical methodologies

Weekly Exams and Personal Doubt clearance system.

Monthly communication of student’s progress to parents.
