Condensed JEE and NEET courses for 12th students.

We are offering a unique course to current 12th-grade students who are aiming to crack JEE and NEET. Our master plan will be to concentrate on the portions which carry more weightage. We will plan a curriculum that seeks to improve question-solving skills. We will have weekly exams, which will enable them to assess their progress continuously, and with our constant assistance On and Off-class, they will stand a high chance to excel in the competitive exams. Our weekly exams will serve the purpose of the question catalog too. As our motive is cost-effective quality education for all, we wish to offer this program at a reasonable price of ₹19,999 for JEE and ₹18,999 for NEET coaching.

Beginnings JEE and NEET Course for 11th students

This course is tailor-made for 11th-grade students who aspire to be at the premier institute for their higher studies. Here we aim to impart superior concept knowledge, vibrant scientific ideas, and strong numerical skills. We will provide materials that will be concise and to the point. We believe that 11th grade is the correct time to enhance the student's aptitude for competitive exams. We will utilize this opportunity to inculcate scientific temper in students, helping them study science with curiosity rather than studying science for necessity. Our weekly exams will be a boon for the students. We offer this package for a price of ₹ 15,999 for JEE coaching and ₹15,999 for NEET coaching(We have vast biology portions to cover thus, this is the reasonable price that we can offer).

Special program for Foreign MBBS students.

We offer a unique program for foreign MBBS students who had an opportunity to join the foreign universities without qualifying NEET this year. We aim to provide an effective curriculum taking into consideration their specific requirement of qualifying NEET. We will adjust the schedule according to the timetable of universities. We will concentrate more on Physics and Chemistry as the first year at the university will have biology courses. We will offer a tailor-made course structure for international students.
